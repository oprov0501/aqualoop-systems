Company History

Founded in 2016 by environmental engineer Olivia Parker, AquaLoop Systems began with a mission to reduce water waste through affordable and sustainable technology. The company's first product was a residential rainwater collection system designed for homeowners seeking environmentally responsible solutions.

Over the years, AquaLoop expanded into commercial irrigation, water monitoring technology, and filtration systems. Today, the company serves thousands of customers while supporting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal of Clean Water and Sanitation.
